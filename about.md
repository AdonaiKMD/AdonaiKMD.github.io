*From Zero to Hero — reaching the frontier, then helping push the next one.*

This blog is an experiment in learning modern AI from first principles — the math, the models, and what comes after. In public, one chapter at a time.

The rule is simple: nothing gets used until it's been derived from scratch. Math, then intuition, then code. If you can't rebuild it from the ground up, you don't really own it yet — you've just memorized a vocabulary.

The plan goes roughly: linear algebra → calculus → probability → neural nets → attention → small transformer-style models, end to end. After that, the parts of AI the field is actively chasing — agents that plan and act, world models, perception across modalities. Foundations first, then the frontier.

The voice is intentionally friendly. This is a beginner gaining confidence and bringing you along — not a textbook, and definitely not an exam. Math is allowed to be fun. Confusion is part of the process. We're figuring this out together.

All code lives on [GitHub](https://github.com/AdonaiKMD).
